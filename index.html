Reflection

My background in Construction Management/Engineering provided me with a strong foundation in problem-solving and attention to detail. However, the transition to tech and taking this class was challenging, yet exciting. Initially, the material felt challenging, primarily due to the shift in required skills This course was a good first step in my career transition, providing both technical skills and a clear understanding of the web development lifecycle.

The structure of this class allowed me to learn quickly. By requiring us to build, debug, and troubleshoot actual projects, this learning style drastically improves the skills I came into this semester with. I enjoyed the direct engagement with the tools to understand the course material.
